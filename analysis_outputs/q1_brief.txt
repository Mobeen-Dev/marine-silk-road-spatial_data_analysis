SECTION 2: MISSING DATASETS - CRITICAL REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

2.1 BASEMAP LAYERS (PRIORITY: CRITICAL)
----------------------------------------
STATUS: ✗ MISSING

REQUIRED DATASETS:

A. World Coastline Boundaries
   Purpose: Visual context for ocean vs. land
   Format: Shapefile (.shp) or GeoJSON
   Resolution: 1:10m or higher
   Sources: 
     - Natural Earth (public domain): naturalearthdata.com/downloads/10m-physical-vectors/
     - GSHHG (Global Self-consistent Hierarchical High-resolution Geography)
   File: ne_10m_coastline.shp or GSHHS_h_L1.shp
   Size: ~50-200 MB
   
   JUSTIFICATION: 
   - Shipping traffic visualization requires land/ocean distinction
   - Helps identify coastal vs. open-ocean routes
   - Essential for map readability

B. Country Boundaries
   Purpose: Political context, EEZ delineation support
   Format: Shapefile or GeoJSON
   Source: Natural Earth
   File: ne_10m_admin_0_countries.shp
   Size: ~20 MB
   
   JUSTIFICATION:
   - Identifies territorial waters
   - Supports geopolitical analysis of shipping lanes
   - Enables country-specific traffic statistics


2.2 OCEAN FEATURES (PRIORITY: HIGH)
------------------------------------
STATUS: ✗ MISSING

REQUIRED DATASETS:

A. Major Ocean Basins
   Purpose: Categorize traffic by ocean region
   Format: Shapefile
   Source: Natural Earth or NOAA
   File: ne_10m_geography_marine_polys.shp
   
   JUSTIFICATION:
   - Essential for regional hotspot analysis
   - Enables "Atlantic vs. Pacific" comparisons
   - Required for marine highway extraction

B. Exclusive Economic Zones (EEZ)
   Purpose: Maritime jurisdiction boundaries
   Format: Shapefile
   Source: Marine Regions (marineregions.org) or VLIZ
   File: World_EEZ_v11.shp
   Size: ~500 MB
   
   JUSTIFICATION:
   - Legal/regulatory context for shipping routes
   - Identifies international vs. territorial waters
   - Useful for policy-oriented analysis


2.3 PORT & INFRASTRUCTURE DATA (PRIORITY: MEDIUM)
--------------------------------------------------
STATUS: ✗ MISSING (but can be derived)

RECOMMENDED DATASETS:

A. World Port Index
   Purpose: Validate hotspot detection against known port locations
   Format: CSV or Shapefile
   Source: National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency (NGA)
   File: UpdatedPub150.csv
   Free: Yes (public domain)
   Size: ~5 MB
   
   JUSTIFICATION:
   - Ground-truth validation for hotspot detection
   - Labels for major traffic convergence points
   - Helps distinguish ports from open-ocean convergence

B. Major Shipping Chokepoints
   Purpose: Identify strategic narrow passages
   Format: Point or polygon shapefile
   Locations: Suez Canal, Panama Canal, Strait of Malacca, 
              Strait of Gibraltar, Bosphorus, etc.
   
   JUSTIFICATION:
   - Expected high-density zones
   - Critical for marine highway analysis
   - Validation reference for detected hotspots
